About Seasoned

Seasoned is a recently launched, well funded startup that is focused on disrupting the high growth, high turnover food service industry. Our goal is to continually improve the lives and livelihoods of the service industry professionals we serve with unmatched access to careers, content and connections. The team is led by an experienced set of consumer technology veterans from LinkedIn, Lynda, Art.com, NoWait, and Home Away who have experience building rich, customer friendly experiences.

No other company seeks to create a place as diverse and dynamic as the industry we serve. With offices in Austin, TX and San Francisco, CA, we�re looking for people excited about being at the heart of how 10% of the American Workforce thrives.

In this role, you will report to the VP of Engineering. You will be an individual contributor, bringing expertise in at least one technology and a demonstrated capacity and the necessary energy (we need fire!) to learn more.

The Stack

We think it�s an interesting one.

  • App: Nginx, Node JS, React-Redux, React Native, GraphQL, Vue, Java, MongoDB, Neo4J, Postgres, Redis
  • Data Infra: Redshift, Airflow, Superset, Python, Java
  • Tools: Jira, Github, Jenkins, Ansible, SumoLogic, AWS (Lamdba, S3, EC2, Route53, Cloudwatch, etc.)
  • Style: Agile, Slack-communicating, Fast, Flexible, Fun

Responsibilities

  • Be an individual contributor to one aspect of the Seasoned Stack (to start). This could include building onto the Web, API, or Data layers.
  • Follow a high standard for Software Development Practices: quality code, test coverage, comments, collaboration, and avoiding shortcuts.
  • Be a willing and enthusiastic participant wherever needed.
  • Learn and learn some more.

Must Haves

  • Proficiency in at least on technical area relevant to the technology stack delineated above. Ideally this is React or Java. If not React, then Angular or some other modern Javascript framework. If not Java, then some other prominent OO language.
  • Basic understanding of RDBMS concepts, especially data structures and SQL syntax
  • Understanding of how web applications work, i.e., what is HTTP? What is a Browser?
  • Demonstrable capacity to learn complex topics and tools quickly
  • Passion and energy to enter the Software Development profession
  • Unafraid to ask questions and challenge mentors when you have a different perspective.
  • Intellectual curiosity

Nice to Haves

  • Direct experience in building a consumer facing application
  • Open Source Project contributions
  • Experience in the Food Service or Hospitality Industry

Last thing: bring a sense of humor and one or two passions outside of your professional interests. We are a team of diverse viewpoints and intellectual approaches, but we all agree humor and a breadth of interests make the team stronger (and more fun)!

One more last thing: we are a dog, bike, Friday cocktails, and snack friendly office.

Benefits: Seasoned takes care of its people, including competitive salaries, commuter benefits, flexible schedule options, 401K matching, excellent healthcare benefits, time off for nonprofit work, and more.

Come join us!
